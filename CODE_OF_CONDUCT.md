#  Code of Conduct

> **RepoXray – X-Ray Repositories, Don’t Just Read Them.**

##  Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **RepoXray**, pledge to make participation in our community a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to fostering an **open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy** community.

---

##  Our Standards

### ✅ Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment

* Being respectful and considerate in communication
* Giving and accepting constructive feedback
* Helping others learn and grow
* Showing empathy and kindness
* Focusing on what is best for the community

---

### ❌ Examples of unacceptable behavior

* Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
* Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments
* Publishing private information without consent
* Spamming, self-promotion without context
* Any behavior that disrupts a safe environment

---

##  Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

* Clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct
* Taking appropriate and fair corrective action
* Removing or editing content that violates guidelines

---

##  Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

* 📩 Email: **[your-email@example.com](mailto:your-email@example.com)**
* Or open a private discussion with maintainers

All reports will be handled **confidentially and respectfully**.

---

##  Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may take actions such as:

* Warning the user
* Temporarily restricting participation
* Removing content
* Banning repeat offenders

---

##  Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub issues
* Discussions
* Pull requests
* Community interactions
